Happy Janmastami!

Teaching's of Bhagavad Gita:-

  • Concentrate On Your Goal Do Your Work And Focus Not On The Result
  • Human Life Is Full Of Battles: Never Shirk In fear – Fight To The Last, Stand Your Ground
  • Identity, Recognize and Accept Equality in Life
  • Desires Will Come To Your Mind And Go
  • A Mind Full Of Thoughts About Money Cannot Concentrate Nor Meditate
  • Whatever Has Happened Is Only For The Good Future. The Activities That Are Happening At Present Are Also For A Good Cause. The Happenings In The Future, They Are Also For A Good Future
  • Change is Permanent. This is Universal Law. You can either become a billionaire or Pauper in a second.
  • Fear Factor is a Disability
  • You Are Born Empty-Handed, And You Will Leave This Mother Earth Empty-Handed
  • Lust, Anger as well Greed – Ways to Self-destruction
  • A Man Makes His Life Based On His Beliefs
